My research focuses on ecological and hydrological processes in dryland and inland river basin systems.

## Research Interests

### Dryland ecosystems and ecosystem resilience

I am interested in how dryland ecosystems respond to climate variability, hydrological change, and human activities, with a particular focus on ecosystem resilience and alternative stable states.

### Watershed hydrology and hydrological connectivity

I study how river networks, lakes, wetlands, and upstream-downstream hydrological processes shape ecological patterns in arid and semi-arid regions.

### Remote sensing and GIS-based spatial analysis

I use remote sensing data and GIS-based methods to quantify vegetation dynamics, hydrological changes, and landscape-scale ecological patterns.
